Impact Training Centre was founded in 2017 by microfinance experts and practitioners as a foremost institute for training and developing financial services practitioners to equip them as professionals in the field of microfinance and other financial services
Our Mision
To provide a practice based training, knowledge and skill for individuals and organizations to enable them provide services in the sector at world class standards, work towards development and growth of the industry and to run profitable and sustainable microfinance institutions that are comparable to others anywhere in the world. To bridge the skill gap in practical training and microfinance skills which is currently lacking in the sector since the introduction of commercial microfinance by CBN in 2005
Our Core Objectives
  • To equip practitioners with practical skills in microfinance for efficient service delivery and optimal performance
  • To provide institutional support for microfinance institutions that lack capacity for self-development due to resource constraints
  • To provide the industry with well-trained human resource pool which has been a major challenge for the industry over the years
  • To complement the effort of the regulatory authorities (CBN and NDIC) in developing the sector and to build adequate capacity for the financial services sector
  • To promote financial literacy to the general public and in particular the numerous financially excluded and underserved population who largely form the target market for Microfinance institutions
